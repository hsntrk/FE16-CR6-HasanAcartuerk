Ristorante Osteria al Peronio

A tradition since 2008

The Osteria al Peronio has been a family-run establishment since 2008, when Dario and his wife Marina decide to take it over, together with their children.

After decades of activity in catering in the province, bringing their tradition to the historic center of Padua.

  • Dario's curiosity in researching and enhancing local products
  • Marina's passion that makes customers feel as if they were immersed in the warmth of home
  • makes the atmosphere intimate and welcoming.

The particular name of the place has a historical significance: the Peronio is in fact the only remaining column of 10 that are part of a complex that supported the canopy that covered the Peroneum a fructibus, the fruit market, in medieval times.
